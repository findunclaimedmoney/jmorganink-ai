----------------------------------------
2. HOW THE TRACKING TAG WORKS
----------------------------------------
Add a tag to the end of the link like this:

    https://YOURSITE/start?v=cars1

The "?v=cars1" part is the tag. It travels WITH the link wherever
you put it — it doesn't matter if it's a video ad, a post, your bio
link, an email, or a banner. Whoever clicks that exact link gets
counted under that tag.

When a lead comes into your inbox, the email tells you which tag it
came from — so you'll know exactly which video or post brought them in.


----------------------------------------
3. GIVE EACH PLACE ITS OWN TAG
----------------------------------------
Use a DIFFERENT tag everywhere so you can tell them apart:

    Video ad #1     ->  https://YOURSITE/start?v=cars1
    Video ad #2     ->  https://YOURSITE/start?v=cars2
    A post          ->  https://YOURSITE/start?v=post1
    Link in bio     ->  https://YOURSITE/start?v=bio

You can name the tags anything you like (keep them short, no spaces).
Example: ?v=super1, ?v=tiktokjune, ?v=homeloans, etc.


----------------------------------------
4. THE ONLY RULE
----------------------------------------
The person must click the FULL link, including the "?v=..." part.

 - If they click the full tagged link  -> the lead shows that tag. 
 - If they just type your plain address -> the lead still comes
   through fine, it just won't have a tag on it.


----------------------------------------
5. TIKTOK TIP (VIDEOS vs POSTS)
----------------------------------------
 - ORGANIC POSTS: TikTok usually does NOT let people tap a link in
   the caption. So for normal posts, put your tagged link in your BIO.
 - ADS: Ads get a tappable button — point that button straight at
   your tagged link.

Both capture the tag perfectly. The only difference is WHERE the
clickable link sits.
